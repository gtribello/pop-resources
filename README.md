The aim of the programme is to show you that mathematics is about more than manipulating formulas and solving by problems by hand. Mathematics is instead a langauge that is 
used by researchers of all types to understand the world. Knowing how to solve the mathematics problems like those you have learned to solve at school is important. However, 
we can use computers to solve many of these problems for us. Getting the computer to do the maths for us frees us to spend more time thinking about the problems 
we need to solve, how to express problems in these mathematical language and how to interpret the solutions to the problems that we have obtained. It is these three activities 
that we will focus on during the summer school.
